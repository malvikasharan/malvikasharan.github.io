Malvika Sharan


Openness and best practices mean different things to different people in different contexts. The many ways of 'being open' and 'practicing openness' should not be taken as restriction or distraction, but as the scientific freedom. We can use this freedom learn, unlearn, challenge, dismantle and rebuild socio-technical infrastructure that prioritises reproducible, ethical and collaborative research for collective benefit.
